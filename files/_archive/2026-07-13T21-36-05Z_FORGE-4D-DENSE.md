## THE TEN GATES

**Every one must be answered ‚Äî in the log, with a number ‚Äî before any result is believed.
"Not applicable" is a legal answer. Silence is not.**

## GATE 1 ¬∑ STATIONARITY ‚Äî *is the thing I am measuring holding still?*
> **The gate that would have saved us.**

Split the history into **‚â•5 sequential windows**. Compute the quantity in each.
- Does the **sign** flip? ‚Üí the estimand is non-stationary. **A single mean is meaningless.**
- Does the **magnitude** hold while the sign flips? ‚Üí **trade the magnitude** (¬ßA3, forge-4d DENSE).
- Report the per-window table. **Always.** No exceptions.

*A mean over a sign-flipping process is zero. We spent a year measuring zero and calling the
market empty.*

## GATE 2 ¬∑ THE NUISANCE ‚Äî *what else could explain this?*
Before believing any effect, remove the obvious confounds:
- **Drift.** De-mean against the *training window's own mean*. Learn deviations, never levels.
- **Volatility.** Normalise by ATR ‚Äî always.
- **Momentum.** The **momentum-matched control**: does this signal beat bars with *identical
  recent movement but no signal*? *(This single test annihilated the entire candlestick canon.)*

## GATE 3 ¬∑ THE SUBSTRATE ‚Äî *does my partition carry information at all?*
Before optimising *within* a bucket, prove **the bucket itself** predicts.
- Measure the IC of the partition alone, out-of-sample.
- **If the substrate scores ~0, everything fitted inside it is noise.**

*Our nine regimes scored **+0.001, p = 0.92**. We optimised 562,500 weight combinations inside a
partition that predicts nothing.*

## GATE 4 ¬∑ THE ORTHOGONAL LABEL ‚Äî *am I grading my own homework?*
The validation label may **never** be constructed from the rule's own inputs.
- If the rule tests EMA200 displacement, the label may not be an EMA200 threshold.
- *Violating this produced a phantom "89% / 0%" that was inflated by **77 points**.*

## GATE 5 ¬∑ REACHABILITY ‚Äî *does this code ever actually run?*
Fire-rate **every** branch and gate.
- `< 0.5%` ‚Üí **dead**. `> 95%` ‚Üí **saturated**; the `if` is decorative and lying.
- Report the constant *and its measured percentile* on this instrument.

*The impulse detector was mathematically unreachable. It had never executed once.*

## GATE 6 ¬∑ POWER ‚Äî *can this sample even answer this question?*
Every measurement is reported **with its minimum detectable effect.**
- `n < 500` ‚Üí the cell may not update a weight. Ever.
- `|measured| < MDE` ‚Üí **the cell produced no information.** Log `UNDERPOWERED`. Never a "weak signal."

## GATE 7 ¬∑ THE UNASKED ‚Äî *what have I not tested?*
**Enumerate the dimensions not examined, in the log, by name.**
- *Day-of-week went untested for the entire programme. It turned out to be **+1.419 ATR on Friday**,
  and a boosted model independently ranked it the single most important feature.*
- **An untested dimension is a silent assumption that it does not matter.** Name them, or you have
  assumed them away.

## GATE 8 ¬∑ THE OBJECTIVE ‚Äî *does the thing I optimise match the thing I want?*
State the objective function and the actual goal side by side. Prove they agree.

*We optimised **win-rate** while the real edge lived at **42% win-rate with winners 1.64√ó losers.**
Our objective function was fighting our own edge.*

## GATE 9 ¬∑ THE INVERSION ‚Äî *is this reliably WRONG?*
A signal with **significantly negative** out-of-sample performance (`t < ‚àí3`) is **not a failure.
It is information, one sign away from being right.**
- **Flag `SIGN-INVERSION`. Never bury it. Interrogate it.**

*Our "catastrophic" ‚àí1.204 ATR was the most valuable measurement in the programme.*

## GATE 10 ¬∑ THE COST ‚Äî *does this survive contact with reality?*
No edge is real until it is **net of measured spread and slippage.**
- A genuine **0.05R** edge is **erased by 0.03R** of friction.
- Until cost is measured on the live instrument, every verdict carries a **`COST-UNVALIDATED`** flag.

# PART IV ‚Äî THE COUNCIL'S DESIGN, AND ITS CATASTROPHE

Armed with real conditional edges, we built the obvious machine: condition on the dimensions that
measured signal, enforce the power floor, shrink by significance, validate out-of-sample.

Every discipline we had ever learned, applied at once.

> **Result: ‚àí1.204 ATR. t = ‚àí11.31. Hit rate 44.4%.**

**It did not merely fail. It inverted ‚Äî reliably, massively, significantly.**

A system that is *reliably wrong* is not broken. **It is a system that has discovered something.**

## The first diagnosis ‚Äî drift
Train window mean forward move: **+0.351 ATR.** Test window: **+0.746 ATR.** The windows have
different drift; a cell estimate that absorbs the training drift is systematically wrong later.

**Basic quant hygiene we had skipped: never learn the level. Learn the deviation.**

So we de-meaned ‚Äî learned only *relative* skill, drift-neutralised, no leakage.

> **Result: ‚àí1.012 ATR. t = ‚àí10.68.**
>
> **Still inverted.**

Drift was not the disease. **Drift was a symptom.**

# PART VII ‚Äî THE ARCHITECTURE THAT SURVIVES A LIVING EDGE

## A1 ¬∑ THE REGIME OF THE EDGE (not of the market)
The market has regimes. **So does the edge itself.** Model the *second-order* process: not "is the
market trending?" but **"is `ASIA √ó cold` currently in its positive phase or its negative phase?"**
Every cell gets its own state machine: `POSITIVE ¬∑ NEGATIVE ¬∑ TRANSITIONING ¬∑ UNKNOWN`. This is
the object nobody has ever modelled, and it is the only object that matters.

## A2 ¬∑ THE FLIP DETECTOR
Learn the *transition dynamics of the sign*, not the level. Measure, per cell: mean phase
duration, historical flip frequency, and the observable precursors of a flip (does volatility
regime shift first? does the cell's variance rise before its mean crosses zero?). **A cell whose
phase is stable and mature is tradeable. A cell approaching a historical flip point is
radioactive.**

## A3 ¬∑ SIGN-AGNOSTIC EDGE ‚Äî *the deepest move*
Notice what never changed in that table: **the magnitude.** `|+3.75|`, `|‚àí1.01|`, `|+2.66|` ‚Äî the
*energy* is persistently large; only the *direction* is unstable.

**Therefore: extract edge from magnitude, not direction.** When a cell is *active* ‚Äî large |move|,
sign uncertain ‚Äî the correct instrument is not a directional bet. It is a **straddle-like posture:
options, or bracketed entries that profit from motion regardless of its sign.** MT4 constrains us,
but bracket-both-sides on high-|magnitude| cells is *directly implementable* and is **immune to
the exact failure that has defeated every version of this system.**

> **We have been betting on direction in a market whose direction is the one thing that will not
> hold still ‚Äî while its magnitude was stable all along.**

## A4 ¬∑ ENSEMBLE OVER PHASES, NOT OVER BARS
Never fit one model on all history. Fit **one model per phase**, and at live time ask: *which
phase does the present most resemble?* This is the Cartographer, but retrieving on **phase
similarity** rather than bar similarity.

## A5 ¬∑ THE INVERSION ALARM
A reliably-inverting signal is **information, not failure.** If out-of-sample performance is
significantly *negative* (t < ‚àí3), the system must **flag SIGN-INVERSION**, not silently fail. It
is then one line from being right. **Never discard an anti-predictive signal. Interrogate it.**

## A6 ¬∑ SURVIVE THE FLIP
Since being wrong about the sign is **inevitable, not exceptional**: position size must assume the
sign may invert *during the trade*. Stops sized from the cell's own historical adverse excursion.
Exposure capped so a full phase-inversion is survivable. **Survival is not a constraint here ‚Äî it
is the core design assumption.**

# PART VIII ‚Äî THE MANDATES OF forge-4d DENSE

**THE LIVING-EDGE MANDATE.** No edge is ever learned once. Every edge is a **process with phases**,
and the system's primary job is to know *which phase it is in* ‚Äî not what the average was.

**THE MAGNITUDE MANDATE.** Where sign is unstable but magnitude is stable, **trade the magnitude.**
Direction is the fragile variable. Energy is the durable one.

**THE INVERSION MANDATE.** A signal that is reliably wrong is a signal that is reliably *informative*.
Flag it, never bury it. Our ‚àí1.2 ATR "catastrophe" was the most valuable measurement in the
programme.

**THE THREE-DIMENSION MANDATE.** Never slice fewer than three conditioning dimensions. The edge
lives in `session √ó displacement √ó volatility √ó day` ‚Äî cells we avoided building because they
looked thin, while the counterfactual multiplier was sitting there to make them thick.

**THE HONEST-FAILURE MANDATE.** Path shape: nothing. Long horizon: nothing. Nonlinear learner:
inverted. **Ship every one of these refutations.** They cost little and they permanently close doors
that would otherwise be reopened by every future optimist ‚Äî including us.

**THE PRE-FLIGHT MANDATE (supreme).** The ten gates of **Part I** run before every pass, and their
answers are logged in numbers. **They are not advisory.** Gate 1 (stationarity) alone would have
saved a year. Gate 3 (substrate) would have saved 562,500 wasted searches. Gate 7 (the unasked)
would have found Friday. **A skipped gate is a P0 defect, and the pass halts.**

**THE INHERITED DISCIPLINE (absolute).** Powered. Shrunk. Replicated. Null-controlled. Net of cost.
Survivable. Train-only. Never validate against a label from its own inputs. Zero-regression. A
stand-down is correct behaviour.
