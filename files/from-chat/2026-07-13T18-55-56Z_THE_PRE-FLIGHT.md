# THE PRE-FLIGHT
## Mandatory Assumption Audit — a prerequisite, not a phase

*Appendix to forge-4d DENSE. This document has priority over every other instruction.
No measurement may be trusted, no weight learned, no verdict believed, until the Pre-Flight
has been run and its answers logged.*

---

# WHY THIS EXISTS

Eight oversights nearly destroyed this programme. Read them as a set:

| # | The oversight | The class of error |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Sign-flip.** We measured a mean and never checked it was *stable*. | *We validated the estimate — never the estimand.* |
| 2 | **Drift.** We learned the level, not the deviation. | *We fitted a quantity contaminated by a nuisance parameter.* |
| 3 | **Hard regimes (IC = 0.001).** | *We never audited our own substrate.* |
| 4 | **Circular validation (89%/0%).** | *We graded our own homework.* |
| 5 | **Dead code (p100 thresholds).** | *We never measured whether our code ran.* |
| 6 | **Thin cells (n = 140).** | *We never asked if the sample could answer the question.* |
| 7 | **Day-of-week, never examined once.** | *We never enumerated what we had not tested.* |
| 8 | **Win-rate objective.** | *We never checked the objective matched the goal.* |

**Every one is the same error wearing a different mask: an assumption that was never given a test.**
Not a wrong answer. **An unasked question.**

> **More rigour on the answers would not have caught a single one of them.**
> This is why the Pre-Flight exists. It does not check our conclusions.
> **It interrogates our assumptions.**

# THE TEN GATES

**Every one must be answered — in the log, with a number — before any result is believed.
"Not applicable" is a legal answer. Silence is not.**

## GATE 1 · STATIONARITY — *is the thing I am measuring holding still?*
> **The gate that would have saved us.**

Split the history into **≥5 sequential windows**. Compute the quantity in each.
- Does the **sign** flip? → the estimand is non-stationary. **A single mean is meaningless.**
- Does the **magnitude** hold while the sign flips? → **trade the magnitude** (§A3, forge-4d DENSE).
- Report the per-window table. **Always.** No exceptions.

*A mean over a sign-flipping process is zero. We spent a year measuring zero and calling the
market empty.*

## GATE 2 · THE NUISANCE — *what else could explain this?*
Before believing any effect, remove the obvious confounds:
- **Drift.** De-mean against the *training window's own mean*. Learn deviations, never levels.
- **Volatility.** Normalise by ATR — always.
- **Momentum.** The **momentum-matched control**: does this signal beat bars with *identical
  recent movement but no signal*? *(This single test annihilated the entire candlestick canon.)*

## GATE 3 · THE SUBSTRATE — *does my partition carry information at all?*
Before optimising *within* a bucket, prove **the bucket itself** predicts.
- Measure the IC of the partition alone, out-of-sample.
- **If the substrate scores ~0, everything fitted inside it is noise.**

*Our nine regimes scored **+0.001, p = 0.92**. We optimised 562,500 weight combinations inside a
partition that predicts nothing.*

## GATE 4 · THE ORTHOGONAL LABEL — *am I grading my own homework?*
The validation label may **never** be constructed from the rule's own inputs.
- If the rule tests EMA200 displacement, the label may not be an EMA200 threshold.
- *Violating this produced a phantom "89% / 0%" that was inflated by **77 points**.*

## GATE 5 · REACHABILITY — *does this code ever actually run?*
Fire-rate **every** branch and gate.
- `< 0.5%` → **dead**. `> 95%` → **saturated**; the `if` is decorative and lying.
- Report the constant *and its measured percentile* on this instrument.

*The impulse detector was mathematically unreachable. It had never executed once.*

## GATE 6 · POWER — *can this sample even answer this question?*
Every measurement is reported **with its minimum detectable effect.**
- `n < 500` → the cell may not update a weight. Ever.
- `|measured| < MDE` → **the cell produced no information.** Log `UNDERPOWERED`. Never a "weak signal."

## GATE 7 · THE UNASKED — *what have I not tested?*
**Enumerate the dimensions not examined, in the log, by name.**
- *Day-of-week went untested for the entire programme. It turned out to be **+1.419 ATR on Friday**,
  and a boosted model independently ranked it the single most important feature.*
- **An untested dimension is a silent assumption that it does not matter.** Name them, or you have
  assumed them away.

## GATE 8 · THE OBJECTIVE — *does the thing I optimise match the thing I want?*
State the objective function and the actual goal side by side. Prove they agree.

*We optimised **win-rate** while the real edge lived at **42% win-rate with winners 1.64× losers.**
Our objective function was fighting our own edge.*

## GATE 9 · THE INVERSION — *is this reliably WRONG?*
A signal with **significantly negative** out-of-sample performance (`t < −3`) is **not a failure.
It is information, one sign away from being right.**
- **Flag `SIGN-INVERSION`. Never bury it. Interrogate it.**

*Our "catastrophic" −1.204 ATR was the most valuable measurement in the programme.*

## GATE 10 · THE COST — *does this survive contact with reality?*
No edge is real until it is **net of measured spread and slippage.**
- A genuine **0.05R** edge is **erased by 0.03R** of friction.
- Until cost is measured on the live instrument, every verdict carries a **`COST-UNVALIDATED`** flag.

---

# THE STANDING LAW

> **No result may be reported, no weight learned, no verdict believed, and no trade armed until all
> ten gates are answered in the log, with numbers.**
>
> **A gate that cannot be answered is a gate that has FAILED.**
> **A gate that is skipped is a defect of the highest severity.**

**And the meta-gate, which supersedes all others:**

> ### GATE Ø · THE UNKNOWN UNKNOWN
> After every pass, ask explicitly and answer in writing:
> **"What assumption am I making that I have not tested — and what would it cost me if it were
> false?"**
>
> All eight of our oversights would have been caught by *asking this question honestly, once.*

# THE VOICE OF THE PRE-FLIGHT

The log must remain **elegant, human, orderly** — the established house style, aligned pipes,
lowercase descriptors, plain nouns, first person, present tense. **It narrates what is happening
now, and nothing that is not.**

```
  PRE-FLIGHT   |   ten gates   |   before I trust a single number
  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  stationarity |   ASIA x cold   +3.75 · +1.61 · −0.63 · −0.56 · +0.51
               |   -> the sign flips. this edge breathes. I will not
               |      trade its direction; I will trade its magnitude.
  nuisance     |   de-meaned against train window (+0.351 ATR removed)
               |   momentum-matched control applied. signal survives.
  substrate    |   regime partition IC +0.001 (p 0.92)   |   it predicts
               |   nothing. I will not fit weights inside it.
  label        |   orthogonal — 100-bar regression R², raw price only
  reachability |   47 gates fired · 3 dead (<0.5%) · 1 saturated (>95%)
  power        |   Strong Bull  n=2,841  MDE 0.082R   |   I can see here
               |   Range        n=612    MDE 0.177R   |   below resolution.
               |   holding prior. learning nothing today, and saying so.
  unasked      |   NOT TESTED: order-flow · news windows · month-of-year
               |   -> named, not assumed away. next pass.
  objective    |   optimising expectancy (not win-rate). they agree.
  inversion    |   none flagged this pass.
  cost         |   spread 25.0 pts = 86% of ATR   |   COST-UNVALIDATED
               |   -> no edge here survives friction. I will not trade.
  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  PRE-FLIGHT   |   10/10 answered   |   2 gates failed   |   HOLDING
```

**Every line is a present-tense account of a present-tense act.** No stale version strings. No
fossils. No description of logic that no longer runs. **If the code no longer does it, the log no
longer says it.**

> **We failed because we never questioned the questions.**
>
> A machine can be rigorous and blind at the same time — and ours was, magnificently, for a year.
> The Pre-Flight is the instrument that looks at the instrument.
>
> **Ten gates. Answered in numbers. Every pass. Forever. No exceptions.**
